# Celebration Surface Implementation

## Summary
- Promoted Family Celebration from secondary goal metadata into a dedicated Celebration Surface with anticipation, ready, and celebrated presentations.
- Connected Motivation storytelling as Goal → Progress → Celebration so the celebration remains tied to family progress and recognition.
- Increased Child Workspace celebration visibility in the hero and Family Goal journey sections, with clear upcoming, ready, and celebrated states.
- Increased Home visibility with a compact celebration surface that preserves summary-first Home behavior while making ReadyToCelebrate difficult to miss.

## Boundaries Preserved
- Family Celebrations continue to use the existing Planned, ReadyToCelebrate, and Celebrated states.
- No Reward Economy, gems, shop, purchases, individual rewards, notifications, celebration history, celebration memory, goal templates, or dashboard customization were added.
- Existing Family Goals, Motivation, Child Workspace, Child Journey, Helpful Moments, and Tasks remain preserved.
