# HomeOps Product Experience Review Round 6 — Child Persona Age 5

## Independent Verdict
I think this now feels like: “I helped!” I would like seeing that my family noticed me. I would like the fun family thing getting closer. I might not explain all the steps, but I would feel included.

## Ownership Assessment
**Strengths**
- It says my help mattered.
- It says my family noticed.
- It says we did it together.
- The celebration sounds like something happy for everyone.

**Weaknesses**
- Some words are big, like contribution.
- I may need a grown-up to say, “this was because you helped.”
- I want to see my own helping moment more clearly when we celebrate.

**Reviewer Verdict:** I feel like I helped if a grown-up reads it with me.

**Score:** Good

## Contribution Assessment
I can understand helping. I care about “my family noticed me.” It feels like a family story when it shows my helpful moment.

**Score:** Good

## Child Experience Assessment
For ages 3–5, the best parts are recognition, belonging, and simple celebration. I understand that I helped and that the family is happy. I may not understand the whole progress path.

**Score:** Good

## Helpful Moments Assessment
Helpful Moments are my favorite part because they say something nice about me.

**Score:** Excellent

## Celebration Assessment
Celebration feels like we earned something together, but I want it to feel bigger and more fun.

**Score:** Good

## Memory Assessment
Memory helps me remember “we did it,” but I care most if it shows me and my family helping.

**Score:** Good

## Family Story Assessment
I understand: help → get closer → celebrate → remember. I need simple words.

**Score:** Good

## Product Identity Assessment
HomeOps feels like a **Family Product** because it is about my family noticing me and doing something together.

**Score:** Good

## Biggest Remaining Problem
I want the app to show exactly what I did when we celebrate.

## Recommended Next Improvement
When the family celebrates, show my helpful moments in a simple, happy way.
