# HomeOps Product Experience Review Round 6 — Child Persona Age 10

## Independent Verdict
I can now understand that I helped the family reach the celebration. It does not feel like only parents completed a goal. It mostly feels like the family made progress because people, including me, contributed.

## Ownership Assessment
**Strengths**
- The story starts with contribution, so my role is clearer.
- Helpful Moments make it feel like my actual behavior matters.
- Celebration is connected to helping, not just completion.
- Memories explain that we celebrated because we helped together.

**Weaknesses**
- I would want clearer proof of which of my actions counted.
- Some wording tells me I mattered, but I want to see it in the celebration history.
- The family goal still feels partly parent-created.

**Reviewer Verdict:** I would probably say “I helped make this happen,” especially if my Helpful Moments are visible.

**Score:** Good

## Contribution Assessment
Contribution is visible and understandable. It feels meaningful when it is tied to specific appreciation notes. It feels more like a family story than a feature.

**Score:** Good

## Child Experience Assessment
For ages 6–12, ownership is understood and mostly felt. I understand how I helped, why it mattered, and what happened because the family helped. I still want more personal evidence.

**Score:** Good

## Helpful Moments Assessment
Helpful Moments reinforce ownership well because they recognize actual behavior. They would be even better if they were grouped into “what made this celebration happen.”

**Score:** Excellent

## Celebration Assessment
Celebration now feels earned together. It would be more motivating if the ready/celebrated moment showed the contributions behind it.

**Score:** Good

## Memory Assessment
Memory answers why the celebration happened, but not always enough detail about who did what.

**Score:** Good

## Family Story Assessment
This is mostly one family story. The weakest connection is from individual contribution to final memory.

**Score:** Good

## Product Identity Assessment
HomeOps is a **Family Product** because it helps kids participate in family progress instead of just showing chores.

**Score:** Good

## Biggest Remaining Problem
The app needs stronger personal evidence: “these were my contributions that helped us get there.”

## Recommended Next Improvement
Add existing Helpful Moments into celebration/memory as the proof of how the family made it happen.
