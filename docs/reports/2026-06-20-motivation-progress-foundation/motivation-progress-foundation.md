# Motivation Progress Foundation

Implemented the first small Tasks → Motivation connection.

- Completed Shared Household tasks now advance the active family goal.
- Completed Family Member tasks now advance active individual goals for the matching member.
- Unassigned tasks, ordinary assigned tasks for other members, Lists, Agenda items, and Motivation actions do not contribute to unrelated goals.
- No goal configuration, reward economy, or gamification behavior was added.
